XLIII
THE COUNCIL OF TRENT, 1545—63

In 1545 the Emperor informed the Pope that the recent Diets
and Colloquies were but a blind to keep the Protestants quiet till
his preparations for war were complete ; and begged him to open
the Council as soon as possible. It met, at first, in a like spirit.
